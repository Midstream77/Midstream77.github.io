Abstract
This simulation project is aiming on evolving random noise into creatures in a 2-d virtual environment
while trying to minimum human’s predene and intervention. Genetic algorithm is used to generate
and evolve the brain’s neural system of the creature, which controls all the individual’s behaviors.
Creatures are allowed to interact with environment and each other, which leads to the natural selection
to happen. After evolving for thousands of generations, creatures shows a certain level of intelligence
to solve simple problems. Similar behavior patterns also been found between virtual creatures group
and the real animals, like escaping from predators and seeking for food. The evolutionary process of
the virtual world also matches the fossil record for evolution in early stage of earth.
